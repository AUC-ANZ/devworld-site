Performance Tips & Tricks with Unity on iOS

Maya Kerr

Unity is and extremely popular tool for iOS development in part due to it's extensive built-in functionality and ease of development. However, the complexity of the underlying frameworks necessary to provide that functionality can make for unintuitive performance quirks, especially when used to developing in Objective C, Swift, or targeting desktop devices. This talk covers how to identify & correct performance issues in Unity apps targeting iOS, examples of common performance pitfalls and how to mitigate them. This talk mixes a presentation of a variety of tips and tricks learned over almost a decade of iOS development in Unity with live demonstration of various profiling tools.

About Maya Kerr

Maya is a freelance Developer & Technical Artist with almost a decade of experience focused on graphics development & performance optimisation for mobile games and apps. She's been squeezing performance out of Unity on iOS since the original iPhone and lead development on multiple award-winning iOS games.
