Let's talk about React Native

Terence Huynh, Localz

An introduction to React Native, covering how it can be used to build iOS (and cross-platform) applications - and when you should (or shouldn't) use it for your next project.

About Terence Huynh

Terence Huynh is currently a Software Engineer at Localz, a startup that is developing last-mile solutions for retail, logistics and utility businesses based in Melbourne, Australia. In addition, he is the founder of UNIHACK, Australia's premier university student hackathon. With 300+ students competing across two cities, it is one of the largest hackathons in Australia.

/dev/world is Australia's longest running conference for Swift, macOS and iOS developers and designers, and returns to Melbourne 2-4 September 2019.
