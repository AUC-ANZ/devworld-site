Dinner Logistics

The /dev/world/2019 dinner will be held at the Melbourne Marriott hotel.

We'll be leading a group walk from the conference venue to the dinner at 6:10PM. Please meet at the conference desk between 6 and 6:10 PM on Tuesday to join the walk. It is an easy 15 minute walk from the conference venue, with minimal incline change.

If you'd prefer to make your own way there, please arrive by 6:30 PM.
